How to use Clash Bot?

All of your scheduling will be done on the Teams page. You can navigate to it using the menu at the top left.

Here are the components and their usage below.

Discord Server Filter

For switching between Servers

This component will show you which Discord Server Clash Teams you are currently viewing.

If you want to change, just use the filter button to swap between your Servers.

The 'Free Agents' option at the top is not restricted to a single Server.

New Team Card

For creating a brand new Clash Tournament Team

To start, you can create a brand new Team for one of the upcoming Clash Tournaments in the Discord Server you have selected.

Just click on the plus sign and select the Tournament and Role you want to sign up for.

Component

Team Cards

For joining existing Clash Tournament Teams

Each Team contains up to 5 players, these are each for a dedicated League of Legends role.

To add yourself to a Team for a given role, just click on the role and then confirm that is the role you want to join with.

To remove yourself from a Team, you can either join another Team or click the minus button next to your name.

Important!

You can only be assigned to a single Team or Tentative Queue per Tournament. That means if you assign yourself to another Team while belonging to another for the same Tournament, you will be removed from the first Team.

Tentative Queue

For letting others know you are unsure if you are able to play

If you are uncertain if you will be able to play in a Clash Tournament, have no fear! You can use this component to let your Server mates know you are tentatively available.

You can use the plus and minus button on the far right of the table to add and remove yourself from the queue.
